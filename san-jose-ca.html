Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see movement in my San Jose visibility?
Most businesses see initial improvements within 2-4 weeks as profile completeness increases. Sustainable growth typically develops over 3-6 months as Google recognizes consistent engagement and relevance signals from San Jose customers.

Can you guarantee Map Pack rankings for competitive San Jose terms?
No ethical provider can guarantee specific rankings. Google's algorithm considers many factors beyond profile optimization, including proximity, relevance, and competition. We focus on maximizing your visibility within what's controllable.

What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require manager-level access to implement optimizations, respond to reviews, and upload content. Owner-level access remains with you, and we never request personal account credentials.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
Properly implemented call tracking uses forwarding numbers that don't affect your primary business listing. We ensure NAP consistency is maintained across all platforms while gaining valuable call insights.

How do you handle profile suspensions?
We immediately pause all optimization, diagnose the suspension cause, and guide you through Google's reinstatement process with proper documentation. Most suspensions relate to guideline violations we help prevent through compliant management.

What if we already rank well in San Jose searches?
Strong rankings create opportunity for further optimization. We focus on converting visibility into more qualified actions, improving customer experience, and protecting your position against competitive threats.

What happens if competitors use spam tactics?
We document violations systematically and file redressals with Google. While resolution timelines vary, consistent reporting of clear violations typically leads to corrective action against spammy competitors.

How often should we post on our Google Business Profile?
We recommend 2-4 posts weekly to maintain engagement with San Jose customers. Quality and relevance matter more than frequency, so we focus on valuable content rather than arbitrary posting schedules.
