No ethical provider can guarantee specific rankings due to Google's constantly changing algorithms and competitive factors. We focus on maximizing what we control: profile completeness, accuracy, and engagement signals that influence visibility.

What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require manager-level access to implement optimizations effectively. This allows us to update information, respond to reviews, add photos, and monitor performance without compromising your primary ownership of the listing.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
We use compliant forwarding numbers that don't affect your core business phone number consistency. Your primary NAP remains unchanged across directories while we track call sources separately.

We immediately pause all optimization, diagnose the suspension cause, gather supporting documentation, and file a detailed reinstatement request. Most suspensions resolve within 2-4 weeks with proper evidence and compliance demonstration.

What if we already rank well in Freeport searches?
Even well-ranking profiles benefit from ongoing optimization to maintain position, improve conversion rates, and defend against competitor efforts. We focus on strengthening your advantages and identifying new opportunities.

What happens if competitors use spam tactics?
We monitor for guideline violations and file formal complaints with evidence when we detect spam. While we can't control competitor actions, we can report violations and strengthen your legitimate profile to compete effectively.

How often should we post updates on our Freeport business profile?
We recommend 2-4 posts weekly to maintain engagement without overwhelming followers. Posts should highlight offers, events, news, or seasonal content relevant to your Freeport customers.
