What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require manager-level access to implement optimizations effectively. This allows us to update information, respond to reviews, add photos, and monitor performance while you maintain full ownership and control.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
Properly implemented call tracking uses forwarding numbers that don't affect your primary business listing. We ensure all tracking maintains NAP consistency across directories and your main business information remains unchanged.
