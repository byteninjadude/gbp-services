Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see movement in my Dearborn Google Business Profile?
Most businesses see initial improvements within 4-6 weeks as profile completeness increases. Significant visibility changes typically occur within 3 months as optimization takes effect and Google's systems update.

Can you guarantee Map Pack rankings for my Dearborn business?
No ethical provider can guarantee specific rankings. Google's algorithm considers many factors including proximity, relevance, and competition. We optimize what we control: your profile's completeness, accuracy, and engagement signals.

What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require manager-level access to update information, respond to reviews, and add photos. Owner-level access remains with you, and we provide transparent activity reporting for all changes made.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency in Dearborn?
When implemented correctly, call tracking maintains NAP consistency by using number forwarding rather than replacement. Your main business number remains published everywhere while tracking numbers appear only on your GBP.

How do you handle Google Business Profile suspensions?
We immediately pause all activity and conduct a full audit to identify suspension causes. Then we prepare detailed reinstatement requests with supporting evidence and guide you through Google's appeal process.

What if we already rank well in Dearborn searches?
Strong rankings present an opportunity to convert more searchers into customers. We optimize your profile to increase action rates, improve customer experience, and protect your position from competitors.

What happens if competitors use spam tactics against our Dearborn business?
We monitor for suspicious activity and file formal complaints with Google when violations are detected. We document evidence thoroughly and pursue resolution through proper channels while maintaining your profile's integrity.

How often should we post on our Google Business Profile?
We recommend 2-3 posts weekly to maintain engagement without overwhelming followers. Posts highlight offers, events, and updates relevant to your Dearborn customers and business seasonality.
