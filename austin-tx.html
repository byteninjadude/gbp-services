How long until I see movement in my Austin business visibility?
Most clients notice increased profile views and actions within 4-8 weeks as optimizations take effect. Full impact typically develops over 3-6 months as Google processes changes and customer behavior adjusts.

Can you guarantee Map Pack rankings for competitive Austin keywords?
No ethical provider can guarantee specific rankings. Google's algorithm considers many factors including proximity, relevance, and competition. We optimize what you control to improve how and when your business appears to local searchers.

What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We need manager-level access to implement optimizations. You retain owner access and full control. We never request password sharing, using Google's proper access protocols.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
We use tracking methods that don't affect your primary business number displayed publicly. Your main phone number remains consistent across all citations, preserving the NAP consistency Google requires.

How do you handle suspended Google Business Profiles?
We immediately pause work, diagnose the suspension cause, gather required documentation, and guide you through Google's reinstatement process. Most Austin business suspensions resolve within 2-4 weeks with proper evidence.

What if we already rank well in Austin searches?
Excellent rankings still benefit from optimization. We focus on converting existing visibility into more calls and customers, protecting your position from competitors, and identifying new opportunities you might be missing.

What happens when competitors use spam tactics?
We document violations thoroughly and file official redressals with Google. While we can't control competitor behavior, we ensure your profile follows guidelines and report clear violations that disadvantage legitimate Austin businesses.
