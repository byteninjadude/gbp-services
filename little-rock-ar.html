No ethical provider can guarantee specific rankings. Google's algorithm considers many factors beyond our control, including user location, search history, and competitor activity. We focus on maximizing the factors you can control: completeness, accuracy, and engagement.

What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require manager-level access to your profile. This allows us to make optimizations while you maintain full ownership. We never request ownership transfer and you can remove our access at any time.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
When implemented properly, call tracking doesn't harm NAP consistency. We use methods that preserve your main business number in your primary listing while tracking calls through secondary numbers or Google's native call history features.

How do you handle Google Business Profile suspensions?
We immediately pause all optimization work and conduct a thorough audit to identify suspension causes. Then we guide you through Google's reinstatement process with detailed documentation and evidence of guideline compliance.

What if my Little Rock business already ranks well?
Even well-ranking profiles benefit from ongoing optimization. We focus on maintaining your position, improving conversion rates, and protecting against competitor movements. Regular updates also help you capitalize on new features and algorithm changes.

What happens if competitors spam my listing?
We monitor for spam activity and file formal redressals with Google. This includes documenting fake reviews, incorrect edits, or other guideline violations. Most spam issues can be resolved within 2-4 weeks with proper evidence submission.
