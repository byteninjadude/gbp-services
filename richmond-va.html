Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see movement in my Richmond Google Business Profile?
Most businesses notice improved profile completeness and initial engagement within 2-4 weeks. Sustained visibility growth typically requires 3-6 months of consistent optimization as Google's systems recognize and reward your improved profile quality.

Can you guarantee Map Pack rankings for my Richmond business?
No ethical provider can guarantee specific rankings since Google's algorithm considers many factors beyond profile optimization. We focus on controlling profile quality factors that influence visibility while acknowledging competition and proximity also affect rankings.

What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require manager-level access to implement optimizations effectively. This allows us to update information, add photos, create posts, and respond to reviews while you maintain full ownership and control of your Richmond business listing.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
Properly implemented call tracking uses dynamic number insertion or secondary numbers that don't replace your primary business number. We ensure NAP consistency is maintained across all citations while still tracking profile-generated calls.

How do you handle suspended Google Business Profiles?
We immediately pause all optimization work and focus on reinstatement, gathering required documentation and following Google's appeal process. Most Richmond business suspensions result from guideline violations that we help identify and correct.

What if my Richmond business already ranks well?
Even well-ranking profiles benefit from ongoing optimization to maintain position, improve conversion rates, and protect against competitor advances. We focus on maximizing the quality of traffic and conversions from your existing visibility.

What happens when competitors use spam tactics?
We monitor for suspicious activity and file redressals with Google when we identify clear violations. While we can't control competitor behavior, we document patterns and pursue appropriate channels to address unfair practices affecting your Richmond business.

How often should we post on our Google Business Profile?
We recommend 2-4 posts weekly for most Richmond businesses, adjusting frequency based on engagement and seasonal opportunities. Consistent posting keeps your profile fresh and provides regular reasons for customers to interact with your business.
