Do call tracking numbers hurt my business's NAP consistency?
Our call tracking implementation uses forwarding numbers that don't replace your primary business phone in directories. This preserves NAP consistency while providing valuable insights into calls generated from your Lancaster Google Business Profile.

What if we already rank well in Lancaster searches?
