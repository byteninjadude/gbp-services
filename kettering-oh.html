Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see movement in my Kettering visibility?
Most businesses see initial improvements within 2-4 weeks as profile completeness increases. Sustainable growth typically develops over 3-6 months as consistency signals strengthen and review volume builds.

Can you guarantee Map Pack rankings for Kettering searches?
No ethical provider can guarantee rankings due to Google's complex algorithm. We focus on factors within our control: complete accurate information, relevant categories, quality photos, and genuine customer engagement that improve visibility.

What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require manager-level access to optimize all profile sections. Owner-level access remains with you, and we never request password sharing - all work occurs through Google's secure access system.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
Properly implemented call tracking uses dynamic number insertion that doesn't affect your primary business number in directories. We ensure NAP consistency is maintained across all citations while tracking call sources.

How do you handle Google Business Profile suspensions?
We immediately pause all optimization, diagnose the suspension cause, gather supporting documentation, and file a detailed reinstatement request. Most suspensions resolve within 2-4 weeks with proper evidence and compliance.

What if we already rank well in Kettering searches?
Even well-ranking profiles benefit from optimization to maintain position, improve conversion rates, and protect against competitor movements. We focus on maximizing the value of your existing visibility through better engagement and conversion optimization.

What happens if competitors use spam tactics?
We document violations with screenshots and evidence, then file official redressals with Google. While resolution isn't immediate, consistent reporting of guideline violations helps maintain fair competition in Kettering search results.
