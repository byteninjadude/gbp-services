Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see movement in my Google Business Profile performance?
Most businesses notice improved profile completeness and some visibility changes within 2-4 weeks. Significant traction in calls and direction requests typically develops over 3-6 months as optimization compounds and Google's systems recognize your improved profile quality.

Can you guarantee Map Pack rankings for my Midland business?
No ethical provider can guarantee specific rankings. Google's algorithm considers many factors including proximity, relevance, and competition. We optimize everything within your control to maximize visibility while following Google's guidelines.

What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require manager-level access to implement optimizations, respond to reviews, and create posts. Owner-level access is preferred for certain verification processes. We use secure authentication and follow strict access protocols.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
When implemented properly, call tracking does not violate NAP consistency. We use methods that redirect to your main business number rather than displaying different numbers across platforms, maintaining the consistency Google requires.

How do you handle Google Business Profile suspensions?
We immediately investigate suspension causes, gather supporting documentation, and file reinstatement requests following Google's guidelines. Most suspensions result from guideline violations that we can identify and correct through proper appeals processes.

What if we already rank well in Midland searches?
Even well-ranking profiles benefit from optimization. We focus on converting visibility into actions, improving customer experience through complete information, and protecting your position through consistent maintenance and competitive monitoring.

What happens if competitors use spam tactics?
We monitor for suspicious competitor activity and file appropriate redressals with Google when we identify clear guideline violations. We focus on building your profile's legitimate strength rather than engaging in spam tactics ourselves.

How often should we post on our Google Business Profile?
We recommend 2-4 posts weekly to maintain profile freshness and engagement. Post frequency may vary based on your business type, seasonality, and available content, but consistency matters more than daily posting for most Midland businesses.
