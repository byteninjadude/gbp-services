Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see movement in my Kent business visibility?
Initial improvements in profile completeness often appear within days. More significant changes in local search visibility and engagement typically take 2-3 months as Google processes optimizations and users interact with the updated profile. This timeline can vary based on your industry and competition in Kent.

Can you guarantee a specific ranking in the local Map Pack?
No ethical provider can guarantee Map Pack rankings. Google's algorithm considers many factors outside our control, like user location and searcher intent. We guarantee we will follow best practices to optimize all the factors we can influence for your Kent business.

What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require 'Manager' access to your profile. This allows us to update information, post content, and respond to reviews while you retain full 'Owner' access and ultimate control. We do not need your personal Google account password.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my business's NAP consistency?
When implemented correctly, no. We use a forwarding number that redirects to your main business line. Your primary local number remains consistent everywhere as your official NAP, preserving consistency for local SEO while enabling call tracking from your GBP.

How do you handle a sudden profile suspension?
We immediately pause all active campaigns, conduct a full audit to identify potential causes, and guide you through Google's reinstatement process, providing all necessary documentation and evidence to support your legitimate Kent business.

What if my Kent business already ranks well organically?
Excellent organic ranking is a great foundation. GBP optimization then works alongside it, capturing additional local searchers directly on Maps, improving conversion rates with a complete profile, and protecting your visibility from competitor moves or guideline changes.

What happens if my competitors use spam tactics?
We monitor the local landscape for Kent. If we identify clear spam violations by competitors that negatively impact your legitimate listing, we document the evidence and file formal complaints with Google on your behalf, advocating for a fair playing field.
