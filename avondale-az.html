We require manager-level access to your Google Business Profile through your Google account. This allows us to make optimizations while you maintain full ownership and control. We never request ownership transfer of your listing.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
Our call tracking implementation uses number forwarding that preserves your main business number everywhere it appears online. This maintains NAP consistency while providing accurate call attribution from your Google Business Profile.

What if we already rank well in Avondale searches?
