Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see movement in my Norfolk business visibility?
Most businesses see initial improvements in profile completeness and some query visibility within 2-4 weeks. More significant changes in local search presence typically take 3-6 months as Google processes all optimizations and builds trust in your updated profile data.

Can you guarantee Map Pack rankings for my Norfolk business?
No ethical provider can guarantee specific rankings. Google's algorithm considers many factors beyond our control, including your physical location, user behavior, and competitor activity. We guarantee we will optimize every aspect of your profile within Google's guidelines to maximize your visibility potential.

What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require Manager or Owner access to your Google Business Profile. This allows us to make necessary optimizations while you maintain full ownership and control. We never ask for personal Google account passwords.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
When implemented correctly with proper forwarding, call tracking numbers do not harm your core NAP consistency. We use specialized implementation methods that maintain your primary business number on your main listing while tracking calls from Google searches separately.

How do you handle Google Business Profile suspensions?
We immediately investigate the cause, gather all supporting documentation about your legitimate Norfolk business, and file a detailed reinstatement request with Google. Most suspensions result from guideline violations that we can identify and correct through our audit process.

What if my Norfolk business already ranks well?
Even well-ranking profiles benefit from optimization. We focus on converting more of your existing visibility into customers, improving the quality of leads, and protecting your rankings from competitor spam or guideline changes that could threaten your position.

What happens if competitors use spam tactics?
We monitor for spam activity and file formal complaints with Google when we find violations. While we cannot control competitor behavior, we document patterns of spam and persistently report them while continuing to optimize your legitimate Norfolk business profile.

Do you work with businesses outside Norfolk, VA?
While we specialize in Norfolk and Hampton Roads, we can serve businesses throughout Virginia remotely. However, our deepest local knowledge and most effective strategies are focused on the Norfolk market and its unique search patterns.
