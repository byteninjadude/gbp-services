We require manager-level access to optimize all profile sections. Owner-level access is preferred for certain advanced features. We never need personal Google account passwords.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency?
When implemented properly, call tracking maintains NAP consistency by forwarding to your main number. We use solutions that preserve local area codes and avoid confusing customers or search engines.
