What access do you need to my Google Business Profile?
We require manager-level access to update information, respond to reviews, and add content. Owner-level access is preferred for full functionality. We never need personal Google account passwords - all access is through proper business profile permissions.

Do call tracking numbers hurt my NAP consistency in Houston?
When implemented correctly using Google-approved methods, call tracking does not violate NAP consistency. We use techniques that maintain your primary business number while tracking conversions through extensions or dedicated landing pages.

How do you handle Google Business Profile suspensions?

What if my Houston business already ranks well?
Even well-ranking profiles benefit from ongoing optimization to maintain position, improve conversion rates, and defend against competitor advances. We focus on maximizing the quality and quantity of customer actions from your existing visibility.

What happens if competitors use spam tactics against my Houston business?
We monitor for spam activity and file formal complaints with Google when violations are detected. We document evidence thoroughly and pursue resolution through proper channels while continuing legitimate optimization of your profile.

How do seasonal changes affect my Houston Google Business Profile?
Houston's distinct seasons and weather patterns significantly impact local search behavior. We adjust content, offers, and service emphasis seasonally to match changing customer needs and search patterns throughout the year.
